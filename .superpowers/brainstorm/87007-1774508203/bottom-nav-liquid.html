Liquid Glass Nav — Which style?

Both are icons-only with iOS 26 Liquid Glass material. Choose the floating feel.

A — Floating Glass Pill

The nav floats above the bottom edge as a compact rounded pill — like iOS 26 Safari/tab bar. Ultra-minimal. Active tab has a glowing green active state.

B — Edge Glass Bar

Full-width frosted glass bar pinned to the bottom — like iOS 26 home bar. Active tab gets a glowing dot indicator. Scan pill is gradient green, slightly larger.
